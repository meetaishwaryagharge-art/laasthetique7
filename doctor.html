Dr. Pradnya Asutkar

MBBS MD – Dermatology Dermatologist & Aesthetic Physician

Dr. Pradnya Asutkar is a qualified Dermatologist and Aesthetic Physician holding an MBBS and MD in Dermatology. With over 12 years of dedicated experience, she specializes in diagnosing and treating a comprehensive range of skin and hair conditions — from acne and pigmentation to hair loss, laser-based treatments, and anti-ageing therapies.

Her practice at La Aesthetique blends advanced clinical dermatology with state-of-the-art aesthetic technology, always guided by an honest, patient-first philosophy. Every treatment plan is tailored to the individual — designed to deliver safe, effective, and lasting results.
