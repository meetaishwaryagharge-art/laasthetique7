Hair Treatments

At La Aesthetique, our hair treatments are designed to address hair fall, thinning, dandruff, and scalp health concerns. Led by Dr. Pradnya Asutkar, we combine advanced diagnostics with proven treatments like PRP hair restoration to help you achieve stronger, healthier, and fuller-looking hair.

Key Benefits of the Treatment

Whether you're dealing with hair fall, pattern baldness, dandruff, or an itchy scalp, our hair treatments are tailored to your specific condition. Every plan begins with a detailed scalp and hair analysis to identify the root cause and recommend the right course of action.

PRP hair restoration therapy
Hair fall & thinning treatment
Dandruff & scalp care
Personalized hair care plans

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer PRP hair restoration, treatments for hair fall and thinning, dandruff and scalp care, and personalized hair care plans based on your scalp and hair analysis.

The number of sessions varies by individual, but most patients undergo a series of sessions spaced a few weeks apart, with visible improvements typically noticeable over a few months. Dr. Pradnya Asutkar will recommend a plan based on your hair and scalp condition.
